PID TOF Efficiency

The TOF matching efficiency is defined as (). A pion rich sample was obtained by looking for K0s decay pions. The sample is very pure in pion content for pT less than about 1.5 GeV/c but highly contaminated above that value (the momentum used in the present analsys ). The sample is still rich in pions with a contamination of ~50% above 2 GeV/c From PDG: K0s mass = 0.497 GeV, tau: 8.956e-11 secs branching ration to pi_plus pi_minus = 69.20 -+ 0.05 %
