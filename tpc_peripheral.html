TPC Efficiency Central Events

The TPC efficiency for 200 GeV AuAu MB collisions was used in this analysis. The data used in this analysis correspond to HT triggered events. Therefore I matched the refmult and z vertex distributions of the MC events to the refmult and z vertex distributions that I obtained in the events used for my final analysis. Pions (protons) of both charges are embedded in real events and subsequently matched to get the reconstruction efficiency.

refmult and z vertex distributions

Reference multiplicity and z vertex distribution of the HT AuAu 2010 data used in this analysys, the embedded data (MB 200 GeV AuAu) and the embedded data used for the efficiency calculations in this page. The data used for the efficiency calculation was chosen so that it matched the distributions of the final data used in this analysis (jet-triggered plus HT bias).
